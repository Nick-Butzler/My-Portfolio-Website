John Deere Capstone Analysis

In collaboration with John Deere, I contributed to enhancing their testing processes using Smart Test technology. We automated tests on various product components, improving efficiency during assembly and post-assembly quality checks. Addressing underutilized data challenges, we developed analytical solutions for streamlined data extraction and transformation. Our work met stakeholder requirements for comprehensive analytics, including test identification and yield calculation, culminating in a user-friendly interface for efficient data consumption and decision-making.
